Our Story

Two worlds.
One convergence.

Faralai emerged from a simple but powerful observation: the world's most valuable organisations don't succeed because they have better data OR better strategy — they win because they've converged both into one relentless intelligence.

Founded by a specialist with over 15 years spanning sports marketing, public relations, communications, performance marketing, partnership marketing and sponsorship CRM — Faralai bridges the gap between high-performance sports business and next-generation AI.

The result is not a consultancy and not a tech company. It's a new category — converging intelligence that fuses human expertise with autonomous AI systems to create outcomes neither could achieve alone.

We operate in English, Spanish and French — working with sports organisations, brands, agencies, SMEs and enterprises across global markets.
